How to Cook Pasta? Existentially.

Before boiling water and tossing in those innocent noodles, ask yourself: *Why* pasta? Is it a cry for comfort? A carb-fueled rebellion against your diet? Once you've grappled with these weighty questions, cooking it becomes secondary. Aim for al dente, a state of perfect resistance, mirroring your own against the universe.
