Why are Eggs Oval? Because Birds Aren't Architects.

Let's be honest, if birds designed buildings, we'd all be living in unstable nests held together with wishful thinking and regurgitated worms. Ovals are the compromise between a perfect sphere (rolls away) and a perfect cube (impossible for a bird to lay without extensive yoga). It's the Goldilocks zone of avian engineering.
