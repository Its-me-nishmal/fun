First, Question the Can.

Before you attempt to breach its metallic shell, consider the can itself. A symbol of human ingenuity, yet also a prison for its contents. Is the desire to open it a true need, or merely a manifestation of societal conditioning? Once you've wrestled with these profound questions (and probably gotten hungry), just use a can opener.
